Hello World!

This is the very first version of The Afterglow SMP website!


The Afterglow SMP is a Hermitcraft-style server, made for a more mature crowd. It’s got all the same core stuff... big builds, a player-driven economy, and long-term worldbuilding... but the vibe is a lot more laid-back and unfiltered. Just build what you want, mess around, and let things naturally evolve over time. It leans into that late-night, slightly chaotic energy, where creativity flows easier and nothing feels too polished or forced. It’s less about being perfect and more about just having fun and seeing where things go, just like a trip!
